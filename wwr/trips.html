What About Camping?

We will provide all necessary camping equipment. This includes a sleeping bag, foam pad, waterproof duffle bag, small waterproof day bag and a tent.

What Gear Should I Bring?

The most effective way to plan for temperature and weather changes in the Canyon is to be prepared for both hot & dry and cold & wet. Here's a detailed list of items you should bring:

  • Headlamp: A hands-free light source for camping and nighttime activities.
  • Rain Gear: Unexpected showers happen. A lightweight, waterproof jacket keeps you dry.
  • Wet Wipes: Maintain freshness and hygiene even without traditional facilities.
  • LHigh-Quality Sandals: Proper footwear is essential for safety and comfort.
  • Optional items: gloves, fleece jacket, towel, bandana, small pillow, hygiene products, camera equipment, book, watercolors, journal and pen
